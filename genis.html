Genis - Vell

Genis-Vell possesses cosmic powers inherited from his father and enhanced through the Nega-Bands

Biography

Genis-Vell was artificially created by Elysius, a Titanian Eternal and Mar-Vell’s lover, using her late partner’s genetic material. To protect him, Elysius accelerated his growth and implanted false memories of a normal childhood. Eventually, Genis discovered his true heritage and took on the Captain Marvel mantle as his son

Superpowers

  • Genis-Vell possesses cosmic powers inherited from his father and enhanced through the Nega-Bands:

    - Cosmic Awareness: Allows him to perceive and understand events across time and space

    - Energy Manipulation: Can generate and project energy blasts

    - Flight: Capable of flying at faster-than-light speeds

    - Superhuman Strength and Durability

    - Enhanced physical capabilities

    - Dimensional Travel: Can access other realities using the Nega-Bands
