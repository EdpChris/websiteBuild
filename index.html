Silver Surfer

Biography

Born as Norrin Radd on the peaceful planet Zenn-La, he sacrificed his freedom to save his world from the cosmic entity Galactus, who devours planets for sustenance. In return, Galactus transformed Norrin into the Silver Surfer, granting him immense cosmic powers and a surfboard-like craft to traverse the universe. Superpowers

    - Space travel at faster-than-light speeds
