Biography

Jonathan Hart is the son of a human scientist and an alien woman from the Contraxian race. After an accident involving "Zero Fluid", an experimental energy source created by his father, Jonathan’s body was permanently transformed—granting him immense energy-based powers. To control his volatile abilities, he crafted a high-tech suit of armor inspired by a jack of hearts playing card, which also became his superhero identity
